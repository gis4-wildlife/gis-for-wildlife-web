Wildlife monitoring

Biologging and GIS

We also work for marine conservation. We operate in the intersection of marine wildlife tracking and Earth Observation. By overlaying biologging trajectories with dynamic ocean layers obtained from satellite sensors such as Sea Surface Temperature and phytoplankton concentration, we reveal how species respond to a changing ocean.

Our dedicated platform, Wildlife Tracker GEO, supports researchers, conservationists, and Marine Protected Area managers with the geospatial tools they need to monitor wildlife movement and make informed decisions for ocean conservation.
